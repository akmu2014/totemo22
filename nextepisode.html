NEXT EPISODE

2nd MINI ALBUM

07-26-2021

AKMU’s NEXT EPISODE marks a new beginning, released four years after SUMMER EPISODE (2017). As their first-ever collaboration album, it features guest artists on every track, including Lee Sun Hee, IU, Zion.T, Beenzino, Choi Jung Hoon of JANNABI, Crush, and Sam Kim. Built around the theme “Beyond Freedom,” the album explores inner freedom—strength that remains steady regardless of circumstance. Through seven tracks, AKMU delivers stories of resilience, comfort, and hope, offering warmth and reassurance to listeners navigating uncertain times.

Track List

  • 전쟁터 (with 이선희)
  • 낙하 (with 아이유)
  • BENCH (with Zion.T)
  • 째깍 째깍 째깍 (with Beenzino)
  • 맞짱 (with 잔나비 최정훈)
  • Stupid love song (with Crush)
  • EVEREST (with Sam Kim)
