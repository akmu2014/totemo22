PLAY

1st FULL ALBUM

04-03-2014

AKMU, the sibling duo of Lee Chanhyuk and Lee Suhyun, first captured attention with their refreshing perspective and natural charm on K-Pop Star. Raised in Mongolia, their unique upbringing shaped a pure and optimistic outlook that resonated instantly with audiences through catchy melodies and relatable stories. Their debut album PLAY embodies the joy of communication through music. Shot in Jeju Island, the album’s visuals and sound reflect an organic, unfiltered sensibility—expressing everyday themes with imagination, warmth, and sincerity. With Chanhyuk’s songwriting and Suhyun’s clear, comforting voice, PLAY offers gentle healing and a breath of fresh air, marking the beginning of AKMU’s distinctive musical journey.
