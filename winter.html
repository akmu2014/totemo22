WINTER

2nd FULL ALBUM

01-03-2017

Following 사춘기 상 (SPRING), released last May, AKMU returns in January 2017 with their full album 사춘기 하 (WINTER)—a New Year’s gift that completes their adolescence series. While SPRING captured moments of emotional change that can arrive at any stage of life, WINTER reflects deeper, maturing emotions shaped by the passage of time. Written like pages from a diary, the album delivers heartfelt and warm stories through eight tracks arranged chronologically, tracing life from birth to reflection. As the final chapter of AKMU’s adolescence, 사춘기 하 (WINTER) offers quiet comfort—wrapping listeners in warmth and inviting them to revisit memories of their own youth, even in the coldest season.
