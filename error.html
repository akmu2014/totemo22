ERROR

1st FULL ALBUM

10-17-2022

Lee Chanhyuk embarks on a new artistic chapter with his first solo full-length album, ERROR—a cohesive narrative told across 11 interconnected tracks. Beginning with a single incident, the album traces regret for the past, contradictions of the present, and desires for the future, revealing Lee Chanhyuk’s raw emotions, philosophical reflections, and organic compositions. The title track Panorama marks the album’s emotional climax, while Last Farewell (feat. Chung Ha) adds a fresh dynamic to the story. With ERROR, Lee Chanhyuk boldly presents his unfiltered inner world, setting high expectations for his journey as a solo artist.
