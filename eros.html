EROS

2nd FULL ALBUM

07-14-2025

Lee Chanhyuk continues to define his singular artistic voice with his second full-length album, EROS. Following ERROR, which traced the narrative of his own death, EROS begins with the loss of another—exploring the emptiness left behind and the emotional shifts born from absence. Through candid confessions, irony, and quiet introspection, Lee Chanhyuk questions love, identity, and the fragile sincerity behind smiles and screens. Moving between cynicism and fragile hope, EROS ultimately embraces imperfection, where pain, regret, and unreachable ideals coexist. Even if a brilliant world shaped by perfect love may never arrive, the album suggests that the longing for such light is reason enough to keep living.
